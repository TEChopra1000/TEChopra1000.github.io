About Me

Education Background

I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I received a B.A. in Economics, Summa Cum Laude with Highest Honors in the Major as well as a minor in Mathematics.

I am currently pursuing a Masters in Computer Sciences from Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) with a specialization in Machine Learning. I am a part-time student, and all of my classes are remote and so I am able to work full time in the San Francisco Bay Area as I complete this degree.

Academically, I am interested in studying the different types of inferences that can be deduced from statistical analysis and from data science such as machine learning and deep learning. Practically, I am interested in programming machines to make inferences using machine learning and deep learning for programmatic decision making and improvements in automation.

Work Experience

As an AI/ML technical writer at AWS I write about new products that use Machine and Deep Learning. I mostly work on documentation for Amazon SageMaker and Amazon Augmented AI.

In this position, I both write technical documentation and create code-examples for customers using tools like Jupyter notebooks to demonstrate how to use products. I have also co-authored a number of blog posts about new AWS AI/ML products that can be found here.

Before working at AWS, I worked as a Lead of Business Development in a boutique real estate brokerage in Santa Cruz, CA. I helped created automated systems for lead acquisition and increased the volume of leads in our sales funnel by 3x. Subsequently, the number of sales at the firm increased by 2x.
